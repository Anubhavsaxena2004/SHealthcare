National Scheme

The world's largest health insurance scheme fully financed by the government. It provides a cover of Rs. 5 lakhs per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization.

Benefits

  • Cashless access to health care services.
  • Covers 3 days of pre-hospitalization.
  • No restriction on family size, age or gender.
